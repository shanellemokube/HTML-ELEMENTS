Facts About Dogs

Dogs have a sense of time, it has been proven they know the difference between an hour and five. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk times

Dogs are known for their loyalty and companionship. They have been domesticated for thousands of years. They come in various breeds, each with unique characteristics and traits.
